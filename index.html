LIGHT BULB SUN

Ellis (Elliot Winter), Fay (Marley Hamilton) and Freddie (Evan Rees) are a group of close friends entering the final stages of college. Fay and Freddie are in a relationship but tension soon build between them as Ellis' true feelings come to fruition. This coming of age story explores the lack of communication between friends, lovers and how being besotted with someone can drive a person to the extreme.

Founded in 2012, Caged Magpie Productions is an independent production company that’s based in Bristol and South Wales. Our work ranges from short films, documentaries including sound documentaries, music videos, music scores and many other filmmaking mediums.

Our aim is to aid the independent creatives who are fresh to the industry as well as continuously generating new and unique work. Everyone at Caged Magpie Productions is proud to share the same beliefs of keeping all our productions professional, personal and pour our undivided attention into each production.

We are always open to collaboration and are keen to meet new and upcoming filmmakers, whatever your skillset, so, please don’t ever hesitate to get in touch below.
